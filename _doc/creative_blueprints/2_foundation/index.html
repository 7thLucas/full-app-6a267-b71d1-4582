The Problem

Paper logs lose pages. Whiteboards get erased. Texts go unanswered.

When a boarding facility relies on scattered manual systems, missed feedings and skipped medications become inevitable. One slip can harm an animal — and shatter an owner's trust overnight.

The Solution

One dashboard. Every pet, every task, every update — accounted for.

PawWatch centralizes check-ins, feeding plans, medication logs, and pickup coordination into a single system. Staff know exactly what to do; managers see everything at a glance; owners get proactive updates instead of anxious silence.
