Core Features
Facility Manager Kennel Attendant Pet Owner
1

Check-In Management

Digital intake with pet profiles — breed, allergies, temperament, emergency contacts, vet info — plus kennel assignment and photo capture at drop-off.

2

Feeding Schedules

Per-pet feeding plans with dietary restrictions, portion sizes, and timing. Staff see shift-based task lists; completed feedings are logged with timestamps.

3

Medication Tracking

Medication records with dosage, frequency, and administration windows. Staff confirm each dose; missed-dose alerts escalate to the facility manager.

4

Pickup Coordination

Scheduled pickup times, owner arrival notifications, checkout checklists for belongings and health summaries, and end-of-stay billing.

5

Owner Updates

Automated daily report cards, real-time photo sharing, and push notifications for key events — checked in, medication given, ready for pickup.

6

Complete Care Logs

Every action timestamped and attributed. Full care records protect the facility from liability and give owners transparent proof of service.
