# PawWatch

## What It Is
PawWatch is a management app for pet boarding facility operators. It centralizes check-ins, feeding schedules, medication tracking, and pickup coordination into one streamlined dashboard — so every pet in care is safe and every owner stays informed.

## Who It's For

### Primary Personas
- **Facility Manager** — Oversees the entire boarding operation. Needs a bird's-eye view of occupancy, staff tasks, and owner communications. Responsible for compliance, safety protocols, and business performance.
- **Kennel Attendant** — Front-line caretaker who handles daily feeding, medication administration, exercise, and check-in/checkout procedures. Needs clear, shift-based task lists they can check off in real time.

### Secondary Persona
- **Pet Owner** — Drops off and picks up their pet. Wants peace of mind through timely updates, photo shares, and transparent care records. Receives notifications but does not manage the facility.

## Positioning
The reliable command center for pet boarding facilities — replacing whiteboards, paper logs, and scattered texts with a single digital system that ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

## Brand & Tone
- **Voice**: Calm, competent, reassuring — like a trusted veterinary professional.
- **Tone**: Warm but precise. Every word should feel like it protects an animal.
- **Visual identity**: Clean teal and warm amber palette. Rounded, friendly UI with clear iconography. Professional enough for a business, approachable enough for pet lovers.
- **Primary color**: #0F766E (deep teal — trust, care)
- **Secondary color**: #F59E0B (warm amber — warmth, attentiveness)
- **Accent color**: #1E40AF (deep blue — authority, reliability)

## Core Features
1. **Check-In Management** — Digital intake with pet profiles (breed, allergies, temperament, emergency contacts, vet info), kennel assignment, and photo capture at drop-off.
2. **Feeding Schedules** — Per-pet feeding plans with dietary restrictions, portion sizes, and timing. Staff see shift-based task lists; completed feedings are logged with timestamps.
3. **Medication Tracking** — Medication records with dosage, frequency, and administration windows. Staff confirm each dose; missed-dose alerts escalate to the facility manager.
4. **Pickup Coordination** — Scheduled pickup times, owner arrival notifications, checkout checklists (belongings, health summary), and end-of-stay billing.
5. **Owner Updates** — Automated daily report cards, real-time photo sharing, and push notifications for key events (checked in, medication given, ready for pickup).

## Scope & Boundaries
- **In scope**: Facility-side operations dashboard, staff task management, pet record keeping, owner notification system.
- **Out of scope (for now)**: Online booking/reservations, payment processing, veterinary telehealth, multi-location enterprise management.

## Strategic Principles
- **Safety first**: Every design decision prioritizes animal welfare. Missed tasks must be impossible to ignore.
- **Owner trust**: Proactive communication reduces anxiety and support calls. Show, don't tell.
- **Staff simplicity**: The attendant interface must work under pressure — large tap targets, minimal navigation, shift-based views.
- **Auditability**: Every action is timestamped and attributed. Complete care logs protect the facility and the pet.
